Usage:
    Connect both or multiple devices to same Wi-Fi network.
    Either one with hotspot and other with Wi-Fi or all on single Wi-Fi router.
    Than scan the QR code or enter the specified link in other device browser.
    Now share the files by uploading them.
    Once uploaded, you can download them on other device.
    There is no limit of uploading files from software, only limitation is your hardware and disk storage.
    There is also no device constraint. 
    This is web-based sharing app so all the devices which have browser can run this webapp.
    No internet connection is required for sharing.
